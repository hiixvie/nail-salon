Our Policy

Appointments & Cancellations

Please arrive 10 minutes before your scheduled appointment. Cancellations must be made at least 24 hours in advance to avoid a fee.

Payment

We accept cash, credit cards, and digital payments. Payment is due at the time of service.

Aftercare

To maintain your nails, avoid using them as tools and apply cuticle oil daily. Return every 2-3 weeks for fills.

Refunds

We stand behind our work. If you experience any issues within 7 days, please contact us for a complimentary repair.
